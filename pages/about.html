About PT PARKES

Your partner in achieving fitness excellence

Meet Your Trainer

Hello, I am Jack Parkes, a dedicated personal trainer passionate about helping you achieve your fitness goals.

I am deeply passionate about fitness, it has truly become an integral part of my life. Over the past year I have lost over 15kg of body fat whilst gaining muscle, this has fueled and transformed my desire to help others achieve their best self. The journey has taught me countless valuable lessons about discipline, consistency and self care. I've discovered powerful tips and tricks along the way, I'm eager to share them with others who are looking to make positive changes to their lives. Whether it's through workouts, nutrition advice or mindset shifts, I'm dedicated to helping people build strength, confidence and ultimately become the best version of themselves.

"Life is about growth. If you don't change, you won't grow"
